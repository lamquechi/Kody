Which voice should the titles speak in?

The body text stays Spectral everywhere (it reads beautifully in long form and carries Vietnamese well). What's in question is the display face — the one used for the hero, story titles, chapter headings and pull-quotes. Below, the same words in four candidates, including a Vietnamese title so you can judge the diacritics. Tell me a number and I'll unify every page to it.
